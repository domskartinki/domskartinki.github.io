Dom s kartinki Pin Scheduler

Dom s kartinki Pin Scheduler is a private internal tool for preparing, approving, scheduling, and publishing organic Pinterest Pins to the owner’s own Pinterest boards.

The tool is used only by the owner of the Pinterest account and is not offered to the public.

It helps organize content from the owner’s own home decor and product content and publish approved Pins to Pinterest.

This application is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest.
